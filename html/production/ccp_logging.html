Pasteurization (HTST)

  • Temperature: 75°C
  • Hold time: 15 seconds
  • High Temperature Short Time
  • Verify before cooling

Homogenization

  • Pressure: 1000-1500 psi
  • Uniform fat distribution
  • After pre-heating (65°C)
  • Cool to 4°C before pasteurization

Temperature Control

  • Chill/Cool/Store: 4°C
  • Pre-heating: 65°C
  • Maintain cold chain
  • Log at each stage
